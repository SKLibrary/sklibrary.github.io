About Our Library

SK Library is more than just a place to study. It is a community hub where students and knowledge enthusiasts come together. We provide a serene space equipped with modern amenities to enhance your learning experience. Our collection includes a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources tailored to meet the needs of every learner.

Whether you're preparing for exams, conducting research, or simply enjoying a good book, SK Library offers a welcoming environment to help you succeed.

Our Facilities

RO Water

Clean and safe drinking water is available at all times. We prioritize your health and ensure the highest quality of water through our advanced RO filtration system.

Quiet Place to Study

A peaceful environment designed to support focused study sessions. Our library is equipped with comfortable seating and adequate lighting to create an ideal study atmosphere.

Well-equipped Discussion Room and Dining Hall

Our discussion rooms are perfect for group study sessions and collaborative work. The dining hall offers a space to relax and enjoy your meals between study breaks.

24/7 CCTV Surveillance

Your safety is our top priority. We have comprehensive CCTV surveillance to ensure a secure environment for all our visitors.

Fully WIFI-Enabled

Stay connected with high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the library. Whether you're researching online or working on projects, our Wi-Fi ensures you remain connected.

Free Papers, Magazines, and Reference Books

Access a diverse range of reading materials including newspapers, magazines, and reference books. Our collection is continuously updated to provide you with current and relevant information.

Fully Air-Conditioned Area

Enjoy a comfortable environment with our fully air-conditioned library space, designed to keep you cool and focused even during hot weather.

Individual Cabins with Charging Points

Our individual study cabins come equipped with charging points and comfortable seating, providing a private space for uninterrupted work and study.

Zero Holidays

We are open every day of the year, including holidays, so you can always find a quiet spot to study whenever you need it.
